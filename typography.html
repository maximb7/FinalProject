Typography Tasks

Axial

I really enjoyed creating this image, after some thorough research I was able to come up with a design in my head that I think has come out very well. Axial means that it all has to be inline with an axis but can be symetrical or asymetrical. So I thought it would be cool to mix up the colours or each side of the axis.

Radial

I found this one slightly more difficult to make as it was hard lining everything up off of the circle. However, once it was complete I thought it turned out very well. Radial means that you need to pick a central focal point where the text comes off, I thought I incorparated this very well in my design.

Dilatational

Once figuring out how to correctly curve the text on a line, I found this design relitvley easy to make. I really enjoyed making this one as I felt I could be a lot more creative with my design. Dilatational is different from radial, rather than radiating out from a point, the text forms a curve around a point. I believe I executed this design very well, and thought the outcome was good.

Random

This one I found a lot more tricky to come up with a design for, as with the random typographic system literally means what it says. Theres no specific rules or alignment, which means you can do pretty much whatever you want. As a result I came up with an intresting design. I do not think it is the most aesthetically pleasing desing, but that could just be because of my taste in art.
